# ISO 27001 Information Security Management - Customer Responsibilities for Cloud Services

## Framework Overview
- **Standard**: ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Information Security Management Systems - Customer Controls
- **Purpose**: Customer responsibilities for information security management when using cloud services
- **Scope**: Controls that organizations must implement when using cloud services
- **Authority**: International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
- **Certification**: Customer organizations can achieve ISO 27001 certification for their ISMS

## Annex A Controls - Customer Implementation

### A.5 Information Security Policies (Customer Governance)

**A.5.1 Policies for Information Security**
- **Control**: Information security policy and topic-specific policies shall be defined, approved by management, published, communicated to and acknowledged by relevant personnel and relevant interested parties
- **Implementation**: Develop, approve, and maintain information security policies with regular review and updates
- **Customer Actions**: Develop security policies, obtain management approval, communicate to workforce, conduct regular reviews
- **Assessment**: Policy documentation review, approval validation, communication verification, review process testing
- **Evidence**: Security policies, approval records, communication documentation, review reports

### A.6 Organization of Information Security (Customer Management)

**A.6.1 Internal Organization**
- **Control**: Roles and responsibilities for information security shall be defined and allocated according to the organization needs
- **Implementation**: Define security roles, assign responsibilities, establish security governance structure
- **Customer Actions**: Define security roles, assign responsibilities, establish governance, conduct regular reviews
- **Assessment**: Role definition review, responsibility validation, governance testing, review verification
- **Evidence**: Role definitions, responsibility matrices, governance documentation, review records

**A.6.2 Mobile Devices and Teleworking**
- **Control**: A policy and supporting security measures shall be adopted to manage the risks introduced by using mobile devices and teleworking
- **Implementation**: Mobile device management and teleworking security policies with technical controls
- **Customer Actions**: Implement MDM solutions, establish teleworking policies, configure security controls
- **Assessment**: MDM testing, policy validation, control verification, compliance monitoring
- **Evidence**: MDM configuration, teleworking policies, security controls, compliance reports

### A.7 Human Resource Security (Customer HR Processes)

**A.7.1 Prior to Employment**
- **Control**: Background verification checks on all candidates for employment shall be carried out in accordance with relevant laws, regulations and ethics and shall be proportional to the business requirements, the classification of the information to be accessed and the perceived risks
- **Implementation**: Background check procedures with verification appropriate to access levels
- **Customer Actions**: Implement background check procedures, conduct appropriate verification, document results
- **Assessment**: Background check validation, verification testing, documentation review
- **Evidence**: Background check procedures, verification records, documentation

**A.7.2 During Employment**
- **Control**: Management shall require personnel to apply information security in accordance with the established policies and procedures of the organization
- **Implementation**: Security awareness training and ongoing security education programs
- **Customer Actions**: Conduct security training, provide ongoing education, monitor compliance
- **Assessment**: Training program review, education validation, compliance testing
- **Evidence**: Training materials, education records, compliance documentation

**A.7.3 Termination and Change of Employment**
- **Control**: Information security responsibilities and duties that remain valid after termination or change of employment shall be defined, enforced and communicated to the person
- **Implementation**: Termination procedures with access removal and security obligation communication
- **Customer Actions**: Implement termination procedures, remove access promptly, communicate ongoing obligations
- **Assessment**: Termination process testing, access removal validation, communication verification
- **Evidence**: Termination procedures, access removal records, communication documentation

### A.8 Asset Management (Customer Data and Application Assets)

**A.8.1 Responsibility for Assets**
- **Control**: Assets associated with information and information processing facilities shall be identified and an inventory of these assets shall be drawn up and maintained
- **Implementation**: Asset inventory management with classification and ownership assignment
- **Customer Actions**: Maintain asset inventory, classify assets, assign ownership, conduct regular reviews
- **Assessment**: Inventory validation, classification testing, ownership verification, review confirmation
- **Evidence**: Asset inventory, classification records, ownership documentation, review reports

**A.8.2 Information Classification**
- **Control**: Information shall be classified in terms of legal requirements, value, criticality and sensitivity to unauthorized disclosure or modification
- **Implementation**: Information classification scheme with handling requirements and protection measures
- **Customer Actions**: Implement classification scheme, classify information, establish handling procedures
- **Assessment**: Classification testing, handling validation, protection verification
- **Evidence**: Classification scheme, classified information records, handling procedures

**A.8.3 Media Handling**
- **Control**: Media shall be handled securely in accordance with the classification scheme adopted by the organization
- **Implementation**: Secure media handling procedures with disposal and transportation controls
- **Customer Actions**: Implement media handling procedures, secure disposal processes, control transportation
- **Assessment**: Handling procedure testing, disposal validation, transportation verification
- **Evidence**: Media handling procedures, disposal records, transportation controls

### A.9 Access Control (Customer Implementation)

**A.9.1 Business Requirements of Access Control**
- **Control**: An access control policy shall be established, documented and reviewed based on business and information security requirements
- **Implementation**: Comprehensive access control policy with business justification and regular review
- **Customer Actions**: Develop access control policy, align with business requirements, conduct regular reviews
- **Assessment**: Policy documentation review, business alignment validation, review process testing
- **Evidence**: Access control policy, business requirements documentation, review records

**A.9.2 User Access Management**
- **Control**: A formal user access provisioning process shall be implemented to assign or revoke access rights for all user types to all systems and services
- **Implementation**: Formal access provisioning process with approval workflows and lifecycle management
- **Customer Actions**: Implement provisioning process, establish approval workflows, manage user lifecycle
- **Assessment**: Provisioning process testing, workflow validation, lifecycle management verification
- **Evidence**: Provisioning procedures, approval records, lifecycle documentation

**A.9.3 User Responsibilities**
- **Control**: Users shall be made aware of their responsibilities for maintaining effective access controls
- **Implementation**: User awareness program with access control responsibilities and accountability measures
- **Customer Actions**: Conduct user awareness training, communicate responsibilities, establish accountability
- **Assessment**: Awareness program review, responsibility communication validation, accountability testing
- **Evidence**: Training materials, communication records, accountability measures

**A.9.4 System and Application Access Control**
- **Control**: Access to systems and applications shall be controlled in accordance with the access control policy
- **Implementation**: Technical access controls with authentication, authorization, and monitoring
- **Customer Actions**: Implement technical controls, configure authentication and authorization, monitor access
- **Assessment**: Technical control testing, authentication validation, authorization verification, monitoring review
- **Evidence**: Access control configuration, authentication settings, authorization records, monitoring logs

### A.10 Cryptography (Customer Implementation)

**A.10.1 Cryptographic Controls**
- **Control**: A policy on the use of cryptographic controls for protection of information shall be developed and implemented
- **Implementation**: Cryptographic policy with encryption standards and key management procedures
- **Customer Actions**: Develop cryptographic policy, implement encryption, establish key management
- **Assessment**: Policy review, encryption testing, key management validation
- **Evidence**: Cryptographic policy, encryption configuration, key management procedures

### A.12 Operations Security (Customer Operational Processes)

**A.12.1 Operational Procedures and Responsibilities**
- **Control**: Operating procedures shall be documented and made available to all users who need them
- **Implementation**: Documented operational procedures with change management and incident handling
- **Customer Actions**: Document procedures, implement change management, establish incident handling
- **Assessment**: Procedure documentation review, change management testing, incident handling validation
- **Evidence**: Operational procedures, change management records, incident handling documentation

**A.12.2 Protection from Malware**
- **Control**: Detection, prevention and recovery controls to protect against malware shall be implemented, combined with appropriate user awareness
- **Implementation**: Anti-malware solutions with detection, prevention, and user awareness programs
- **Customer Actions**: Implement anti-malware solutions, configure detection and prevention, conduct user awareness
- **Assessment**: Anti-malware testing, detection validation, prevention verification, awareness review
- **Evidence**: Anti-malware configuration, detection logs, prevention records, awareness materials

**A.12.3 Backup**
- **Control**: Backup copies of information, software and system images shall be taken and tested regularly in accordance with an agreed backup policy
- **Implementation**: Comprehensive backup procedures with regular testing and recovery validation
- **Customer Actions**: Implement backup procedures, conduct regular testing, validate recovery capabilities
- **Assessment**: Backup testing, recovery validation, procedure verification
- **Evidence**: Backup procedures, test results, recovery documentation

**A.12.4 Logging and Monitoring**
- **Control**: Event logs recording user activities, exceptions, faults and information security events shall be produced, kept and regularly reviewed
- **Implementation**: Comprehensive logging and monitoring with regular review and analysis
- **Customer Actions**: Configure logging, implement monitoring, conduct regular reviews, analyze events
- **Assessment**: Logging configuration testing, monitoring validation, review verification, analysis confirmation
- **Evidence**: Logging configuration, monitoring procedures, review records, analysis reports

**A.12.5 Control of Operational Software**
- **Control**: Procedures shall be implemented to control the installation of software on operational systems
- **Implementation**: Software installation controls with approval processes and change management
- **Customer Actions**: Implement installation controls, establish approval processes, manage software changes
- **Assessment**: Installation control testing, approval process validation, change management verification
- **Evidence**: Installation procedures, approval records, change management documentation

**A.12.6 Technical Vulnerability Management**
- **Control**: Information about technical vulnerabilities of information systems being used shall be obtained in a timely fashion, the organization's exposure to such vulnerabilities evaluated and appropriate measures taken to address the associated risk
- **Implementation**: Vulnerability management program with scanning, assessment, and remediation
- **Customer Actions**: Implement vulnerability scanning, conduct risk assessments, remediate vulnerabilities
- **Assessment**: Vulnerability management testing, scanning validation, assessment verification, remediation confirmation
- **Evidence**: Vulnerability procedures, scan results, risk assessments, remediation records

### A.13 Communications Security (Customer Network and Communication Controls)

**A.13.1 Network Security Management**
- **Control**: Networks shall be managed and controlled to protect information in systems and applications
- **Implementation**: Network security controls with segmentation, monitoring, and access restrictions
- **Customer Actions**: Implement network controls, configure segmentation, monitor network traffic, restrict access
- **Assessment**: Network control testing, segmentation validation, monitoring verification, access restriction confirmation
- **Evidence**: Network configuration, segmentation documentation, monitoring logs, access controls

**A.13.2 Information Transfer**
- **Control**: Information transfer within the organization and with any external party shall be controlled
- **Implementation**: Information transfer controls with encryption, secure protocols, and transfer agreements
- **Customer Actions**: Implement transfer controls, configure encryption, use secure protocols, establish agreements
- **Assessment**: Transfer control testing, encryption validation, protocol verification, agreement review
- **Evidence**: Transfer procedures, encryption configuration, protocol settings, transfer agreements

### A.14 System Acquisition, Development and Maintenance (Customer Development Controls)

**A.14.1 Security Requirements of Information Systems**
- **Control**: Information security requirements shall be included in the requirements for new information systems or enhancements to existing information systems
- **Implementation**: Security requirements integration in system development lifecycle
- **Customer Actions**: Include security requirements, conduct security reviews, validate implementation
- **Assessment**: Requirements review, security validation, implementation testing
- **Evidence**: Security requirements, review records, validation documentation

**A.14.2 Security in Development and Support Processes**
- **Control**: Rules for the secure development of software and systems shall be established and applied
- **Implementation**: Secure development practices with code review, testing, and security validation
- **Customer Actions**: Implement secure development practices, conduct code reviews, perform security testing
- **Assessment**: Development practice review, code review validation, security testing verification
- **Evidence**: Development procedures, code review records, security test results

### A.15 Supplier Relationships (Customer Vendor Management)

**A.15.1 Information Security in Supplier Relationships**
- **Control**: Information security requirements for mitigating the risks associated with supplier's access to the organization's assets shall be agreed with the supplier and documented
- **Implementation**: Supplier security requirements with agreements, assessments, and monitoring
- **Customer Actions**: Establish supplier requirements, negotiate agreements, conduct assessments, monitor compliance
- **Assessment**: Supplier requirement review, agreement validation, assessment verification, monitoring confirmation
- **Evidence**: Supplier requirements, security agreements, assessment reports, monitoring records

**A.15.2 Supplier Service Delivery Management**
- **Control**: The delivery of supplier services shall be monitored, reviewed and audited regularly
- **Implementation**: Supplier performance monitoring with regular reviews and compliance audits
- **Customer Actions**: Monitor supplier performance, conduct regular reviews, perform compliance audits
- **Assessment**: Performance monitoring validation, review verification, audit confirmation
- **Evidence**: Performance reports, review records, audit documentation

### A.16 Information Security Incident Management (Customer Incident Response)

**A.16.1 Management of Information Security Incidents and Improvements**
- **Control**: A consistent and effective approach to the management of information security incidents, including communication on security events and weaknesses, shall be ensured
- **Implementation**: Incident management procedures with detection, response, recovery, and lessons learned
- **Customer Actions**: Implement incident procedures, establish response team, conduct post-incident reviews
- **Assessment**: Incident procedure testing, response validation, recovery verification, review confirmation
- **Evidence**: Incident procedures, response records, recovery documentation, review reports

### A.17 Information Security Aspects of Business Continuity Management (Customer Continuity)

**A.17.1 Information Security Continuity**
- **Control**: Information security continuity shall be embedded in the organization's business continuity management systems
- **Implementation**: Business continuity planning with information security considerations and testing
- **Customer Actions**: Develop continuity plans, include security considerations, conduct regular testing
- **Assessment**: Continuity plan review, security integration validation, testing verification
- **Evidence**: Continuity plans, security documentation, test results

**A.17.2 Redundancies**
- **Control**: Information processing facilities shall be implemented with redundancy sufficient to meet availability requirements
- **Implementation**: Redundancy planning with backup systems and failover capabilities
- **Customer Actions**: Implement redundancy, configure backup systems, test failover capabilities
- **Assessment**: Redundancy testing, backup validation, failover verification
- **Evidence**: Redundancy documentation, backup configuration, failover test results

### A.18 Compliance (Customer Compliance Management)

**A.18.1 Compliance with Legal and Contractual Requirements**
- **Control**: All relevant legislative, statutory, regulatory, contractual requirements and the organization's approach to meet these requirements shall be explicitly identified, documented and kept up to date for each information system and the organization
- **Implementation**: Compliance management program with requirement identification, gap analysis, and remediation
- **Customer Actions**: Identify compliance requirements, conduct gap analysis, implement remediation measures
- **Assessment**: Compliance program review, requirement validation, gap analysis verification, remediation confirmation
- **Evidence**: Compliance documentation, requirement lists, gap analyses, remediation records

**A.18.2 Information Security Reviews**
- **Control**: The organization's approach to managing information security and its implementation shall be reviewed independently at planned intervals or when significant changes occur
- **Implementation**: Independent security reviews with assessment, reporting, and remediation tracking
- **Customer Actions**: Conduct independent reviews, assess implementation, track remediation activities
- **Assessment**: Review process validation, assessment verification, remediation tracking confirmation
- **Evidence**: Review procedures, assessment reports, remediation tracking records

## Assessment Methodology - Customer ISMS

### ISO 27001 Certification Approach for Customer ISMS
- **ISMS Documentation**: Customer information security management system documentation
- **Risk Assessment**: Customer risk assessment and treatment processes
- **Control Implementation**: Customer implementation of selected Annex A controls
- **Management Review**: Customer management review and continual improvement processes

### Common Customer Implementation Gaps
- **Risk Management**: Inadequate risk assessment and treatment processes
- **Access Control**: Insufficient access management and user lifecycle processes
- **Incident Management**: Lack of formal incident response procedures
- **Supplier Management**: Inadequate supplier security assessment and monitoring
- **Compliance**: Insufficient compliance monitoring and gap remediation

### Customer Remediation Priorities
1. **Critical**: Risk management, access controls, and incident response
2. **High**: Asset management, cryptography, and vulnerability management
3. **Medium**: Supplier management, business continuity, and compliance
4. **Low**: Policy documentation and training enhancements

## AWS Shared Responsibility Context

### Customer Responsibilities (Covered in this framework)
- **Information Security Management System**: ISMS implementation and management
- **Risk Management**: Risk assessment, treatment, and monitoring
- **Access Control**: User access management and technical controls
- **Data Protection**: Information classification and cryptographic controls
- **Operational Security**: Security operations and incident management
- **Compliance**: Regulatory compliance and security reviews

### AWS Responsibilities (Not covered - handled by AWS)
- **Physical Security**: Data center physical security and environmental controls
- **Infrastructure Security**: Hardware security and network infrastructure protection
- **Service Security**: AWS service security and platform hardening
- **Compliance Certifications**: AWS ISO 27001 certification and attestations

This framework focuses exclusively on ISO 27001 Annex A controls that customers must implement when using AWS cloud services as part of their Information Security Management System.
